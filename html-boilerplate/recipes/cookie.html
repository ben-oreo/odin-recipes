Mom's Secret Cookie Recipe

Description

These chewy chocolate cookies are perfect for any occasion! But best of all - they're so darn simple to make

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup margarine
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2t vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa
  • 1/2t baking soda
  • 1/2t salt

Steps

  1. Creame margarine and sugar
  2. Add eggs and vanilla
  3. Add dry ingredients
  4. Roll them into balls and roll in sugar
  5. Bake at 180 degrees C for 8-10 minutes

Note: Best the first couple of days
